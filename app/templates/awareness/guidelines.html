Clean Madurai Guidelines

Madurai is a historic and spiritual city. Every citizen and visitor shares responsibility in preserving its cleanliness and beauty.

Do's – Responsible Actions

  • Segregate wet and dry waste
  • Use public dustbins properly
  • Participate in clean-up drives
  • Report garbage via the app
  • Use reusable bags
  • Maintain temple & heritage cleanliness
  • Encourage others to keep clean
  • Dispose food waste properly
  • Maintain sanitation practices
  • Support eco-friendly initiatives

Don'ts – Avoid These Practices

  • Do not litter in public places
  • Avoid plastic dumping
  • Do not spit in public areas
  • Do not pollute water bodies
  • Avoid burning garbage
  • Do not damage public property
  • Avoid food wastage
  • Do not block drainage
  • Do not ignore overflowing bins
  • Avoid single-use plastics

Smart Clean Madurai Initiative

  • Digital complaint reporting
  • Volunteer vehicle coordination
  • Carbon credit rewards
  • Public awareness campaigns
Together We Can Keep Madurai Clean

Clean surroundings create a healthy city. Let us take responsibility and build a greener future.
